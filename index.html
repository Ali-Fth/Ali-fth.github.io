Research Interests

My research spans multiple disciplines, combining neuroscience, machine learning, and biomedical engineering to develop innovative solutions for neurological disorders.

Neural Decoding

Developing algorithms to interpret neural activity patterns and translate them into meaningful commands for prosthetic devices.

Brain-Computer Interfaces

Creating direct communication pathways between the brain and external devices to restore lost motor functions.

Machine Learning in Neuroscience

Applying deep learning techniques to analyze large-scale neural datasets and uncover hidden patterns in brain activity.

Featured Project

My most significant research project pushing the boundaries of medical AI.

End-Net: A Deep Neural Network for Early Detection of Multiple Neurological Disorders from MRI

Ongoing

Developed End-Net, a 24-layer deep learning framework that leverages convolutional blocks and optimized Inception modules to extract multi-scale features from MRI scans. End-Net achieves 95.4% accuracy, 95.5% precision, and 95.3% recall for early detection of Alzheimer's, multiple sclerosis, and brain tumor diseases.

Key Features:

  • 95.4% accuracy in multi-class classification of three neurological disorders.
  • Unified early-stage multi-class classifier for Alzheimer's, Parkinson's, and MS.
  • Explainable AI components for clinical interpretability
  • Processing time under 30 seconds per scan
